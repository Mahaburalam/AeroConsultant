About Us

We at Aero Consultant have a network of local consultant in more than 100 countries for aircraft transition and in more than 30 countries for helicopter transition. Our local consultant network will support you with physical inspection, records review & scanning, annual audit, prepurchase inspection as well as redelivery of aircraft & helicopter.

Our business partnership with CAMOs, MROs and Ferry Companies enabled us to provide complete aircraft transition service under one umbrella.

We are committed to digitalised the whole transition process, right from getting a consultant to delivering the project.
